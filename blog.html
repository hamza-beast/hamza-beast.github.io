Arm Wrestling Training Guide by Mr Pakistan Champion Hamza Beast

Arm wrestling is more than just strength — it's technique, strategy, and explosive power. As Mr Pakistan Arm Wrestling Champion 2022 and 3x Mr Chakwal, Hamza Beast shares the training secrets that built a national champion.

Essential Exercises for Arm Wrestling

  • Wrist Curls & Pronation: The foundation of arm wrestling power. Hamza Beast recommends 4 sets of 12-15 reps with controlled tempo.
  • Heavy Hammer Curls: Builds the brachioradialis — the muscle that gives you crushing grip power on the table.
  • Cable Pronation/Supination: Mimics the exact arm wrestling movement pattern for sport-specific strength.
  • Static Holds & Grip Training: Holding heavy weights for time builds the endurance needed for long matches.
  • Top Roll Practice: The signature move that Hamza Beast used to dominate national competition.

Hamza Beast's Competition Strategy

Winning arm wrestling matches isn't just about being stronger. Coach Hamza Beast teaches students to read opponents, control the grip setup, and execute technique under pressure. These mental and tactical skills are what separate recreational arm wrestlers from champions.

At Hamza Beast Elite Academy, arm wrestling students get access to the same training protocols that produced a Mr Pakistan champion — customized for their weight class and experience level.

Fat Loss vs Muscle Gain: Which Hamza Beast Program Should You Choose?

One of the most common questions Coach Hamza Beast gets from new clients: "Should I cut fat first or build muscle?" Here's the definitive answer based on 500+ client transformations.

Choose Fat Loss If:

  • Your body fat is above 20% (men) or 30% (women)
  • You can't see muscle definition even when flexing
  • You feel sluggish and low energy throughout the day
  • Health markers (blood pressure, sugar) are concerning

Choose Muscle Gain If:

  • Your body fat is already below 18% (men) or 25% (women)
  • You look "skinny" even after training for months
  • You want to compete in bodybuilding or arm wrestling
  • You want a bigger, more muscular physique

At Hamza Beast Elite Academy, we don't force clients into one path. During your free consultation, Coach Hamza Beast analyzes your body composition, training history, and goals to recommend the perfect program.
